Our Story
Founded over 25 years ago, we’ve grown from a small education hub in Mumbai to a leading education revolution.

Mr. Prashant Ganger has been providing students of Mumbai with quality education since 1992. He was the pioneer who brought the concept of Home Tuitions in mumbai and has established a strong credibility for himself in the same field. Having all his years of experience by his side Mr. Prashant decided to take a step further. It was in the month of May 2005, that Wisdom Academy was started under the dynamic leadership of academician Mr. Prashant Gangar. He aimed to provide classroom coaching to hundreds of aspirants.

In the journey of 8 years, Wisdom Academy has grown from strength to strength. What started as a single institute has grown to 2 institutes with more than 1000 students taught. Mr. Prashant has always focused on adapting with time and provide all the help and support to students so that they achieve nothing but the best in their professional careers. Wisdom Academy is a result-oriented institute which believes in giving top results in all competitive exams.
